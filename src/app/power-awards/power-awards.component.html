Celebrating Excellence in the Energy Sector

The Power Awards, hosted as part of the IPPAI Conference, recognize outstanding contributions and innovative achievements in India's energy sector. These awards celebrate individuals, organizations, and initiatives that are driving change, fostering sustainability, and advancing the nation’s energy transition.
